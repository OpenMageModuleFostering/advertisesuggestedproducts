Activating Adverti.se Suggested Products

Thank you for installing our Suggested Products module.

Suggested Products is a collection of three separate modules that individually suggest Related Products, Upsell Products and Cross-sell Products.

Any combination of these modules can be active on your store at the same time - one, two or all three at the same time.

Before any suggested products will appear in the front end of your site you need to complete some simple modifications to your current theme's template files.
